Getting Started
1. Discovery Call
First, we schedule a complementary discovery call to get to know each other. During this call, I learn more about where you’re at and what you’re looking to create in your life and business.

You also have an opportunity to learn more about my coaching practice and service offering. Together, we’ll design a customized coaching relationship that would be most supportive for you.
2. Proposal & Questionnaire
After our discovery call, I send you a proposal with more details about our coaching relationship including timelines, cadence, and pricing.

After we’re signed off and ready to go, you’ll complete an explorative questionnaire so we both have a comprehensive understanding of your aims and aspirations in your life and business before we begin our weekly sessions.
3. Expansive Coaching
Our coaching calls typically happen over the phone or via Skype. With most of my clients, we connect for a one hour call every week. However, some clients prefer to connect twice a month for a two-hour session.

I am passionate about forming meaningful relationships in the world of business and entrepreneurship. Our coaching relationship acts as your foundational support during this transitional time.

FAQ
What kind of experience do you bring to the table as a coach?

I am a strategic business coach with an incredible passion for what I do. I have extensive experience accelerating over 30 businesses and brands, including Visa, Nissan, Clorox, Travel Alberta, the Alberta Ballet, and the Calgary Stampede. I have consulted on a range of enterprises from large multinationals to local businesses and start-ups. I have also experienced the life-changing shift from employee to entrepreneur. I know firsthand the difference it makes to have the right support and resources in place.
Do you offer coaching programs for more seasoned entrepreneurs?

Absolutely! When you grow and scale your business as an entrepreneur, new levels of resourcing and support are a must. You may have questions or hesitations around entering new markets, expanding your product lines, or bringing on an investor. You may be feeling overwhelmed or burnt out after a few years in business. I provide you with tools and guidance as you expand, while ensuring you feel deeply supported as you move into the next phase.
What is the typical length of a coaching relationship?

The duration of our relationship will vary depending on the kind of coaching support you require. I recommend all of my clients begin with a six month commitment, as this leads to the most effective results.
What kinds of things will we cover during our coaching sessions?

You are the ultimate architect when it comes to your life, and the same applies to our sessions. Our coaching calls will begin with a discussion around your coaching requests for the week and then we hit the ground running. We cover the full spectrum and life and business.
What does a blend of life and business coaching actually look like?

I believe that building a successful business requires tremendous personal development. As a life coach, I ask incisive questions and share my insight to help you resolve blocks and embrace your full potential. As a business coach, I provide consultation that moves your business forward including: strategic guidance, transition planning, revenue generation, and customer acquisition.
How do I know if your coaching style is right for me?

During our discovery call, you’ll get a good sense of my approach to life and business coaching and you can ask me questions to ensure my style aligns with your needs. If I had to describe my approach to coaching in two words, it would be holistic and expansive. Successful businesses are born from sound strategy and the entrepreneur’s capacity to fully embrace their potential, move forward with confidence in their business, and cultivate meaningful and supportive practices.
